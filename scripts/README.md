#' Key aspects of surveillance data analysis:
#' 
#' - Descriptive Epidemiology:
#'   Analyzing data by person, place, and time helps 
#'   characterize disease patterns and identify potential risk factors. 
#'   This often involves using tables, graphs, and percentages to visualize the data. 
#' - Trend Analysis:
#'   Analyzing data over time helps identify trends, such as increases or decreases 
#'   in disease incidence, which can be crucial for early outbreak detection and 
#'   monitoring disease control programs. 
#' - Outbreak Detection:
#'   Analyzing surveillance data can help identify unusual clusters or increases 
#'   in disease cases, which may indicate an outbreak. 
#' - Risk Factor Identification:
#'   Analyzing data can help identify factors that increase the risk of 
#'   developing a disease or experiencing complications. 
#' - Intervention Evaluation:
#'   Surveillance data can be used to assess the effectiveness of public health 
#'   interventions aimed at preventing or controlling disease. 
#'
